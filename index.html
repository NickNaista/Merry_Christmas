Merry Christmas and Happy New Year!

It seems that Christmas time is here once again, and it is time again to bring in the New Year. We wish the merriest of Christmas to you and your loved ones, and we wish you happiness and prosperity in the year ahead.

They say the best of all gifts around any Christmas tree is the presence of a happy family all wrapped up in each other. Wishing you a very Merry Christmas surrounded by your precious family, and many blessings for the coming year.

May the good times and treasures of the present become the golden memories of tomorrow for your lovely family, just as our childhood memories of Holidays past are such happy memories now. Let our children see the child within us during Christmas. So that they know that Christmas is always exciting no matter the age.

When you are weak, may Christ be your strength. And if you are sad, may His love bring joy to your heart. When you are in despair, may you find comfort in the love of Jesus.

I hope this season fills you with faith, renewed hope and good health that will last you a lifetime. Faith makes all things possible, Hope makes all things work and Love makes all things beautiful. May you have all the three for this New Year.

Merry Christmas and Happy New Year!

Ian Matthew Valentine Nicholas
